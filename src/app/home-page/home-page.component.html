ABOUT COMPANY
Aorta Systems offers a full range of end-to-end Salesforce solutions, product development and various Salesforce consulting services from implementation, customization and administration to automation, development, enhancements and integrations. We have a very highly skilled and experienced team – certified Salesforce.com consultants, developers, administrators, QA experts, project managers & business analysts, all of whom are constantly supported by our Salesforce Architects.
Aorta Systems was launched in 2013. Headquartered in New York, the company’s development and PMBA centers are also located in San Francisco, Utah, Toronto, and across Eastern Europe. The unique combination of the company’s multi-year CRM engineering experience in conjunction with solid SaaS operations and management skills enables innovation and a wealth of ideas that helps make our clients more effective and productive. At Aorta Systems, the most valuable asset we have is the relationship we hold within our team and customers.
